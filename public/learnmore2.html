Features

  • Manage your client list, including phone numbers, email addresses, etc.
  • See clients at multiple venues.
  • Full tracking of clients' referral histories.
  • Diary and appointment management via on-screen calendars.
  • Automatic mailmerge from your own documents for, eg. appointment letters, etc.
  • Full email integration.

Security

As counsellors, we know that confidentiality and security of your client's information is paramount. That's why our systems work only over encrypted Internet links, and use industrial-grade login and password protection features. You can control which of your staff have access to client information, and, for your additional security, client names and details are stored encrypted on our database, so that they cannot be identified outside your account.
